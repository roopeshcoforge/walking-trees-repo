About WalkingTrees & Our Location

WalkingTrees is your premier destination for authentic water sports and serene event hosting in Nedungolam, Paravoor, Kollam. We are passionate about showcasing the untouched natural beauty of Kerala's backwaters, offering unique experiences that connect you deeply with nature. From exhilarating kayaking adventures through hidden mangrove tunnels to tranquil country boat rides and exclusive private pool sessions, we cater to all preferences.

Our commitment extends to providing a perfect venue for your special occasions, be it intimate ceremonies, lively parties, or inspiring corporate retreats, all set against the breathtaking backdrop of Nedungolam's landscapes. We pride ourselves on delivering memorable moments and exceptional service, ensuring every visit to WalkingTrees is an experience to cherish.
